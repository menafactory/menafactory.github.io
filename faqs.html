FAQs

How do I submit a factory campaign?

Go to the founder page, fill in focus, capital sought, and upload your deck/plan. Our team reviews in 2–3 business days.

How do investors access decks?

Investors register, pass basic verification, and unlock contact/download links in the project cards.

What fees apply?

Fees are presented per campaign and depend on instrument (equity/convertible/revenue share). Contact us for details.
